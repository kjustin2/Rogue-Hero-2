# Rogue Hero 2

## Tagline

**Ride the tempo. Crash together. Die laughing.**

## Description

Rogue Hero 2 is a tempo-driven roguelike deck-builder where every fight is a tightrope walk between fury and freezeout. Your damage, your speed, even your dodge all hinge on a single 0–100 Tempo meter — push it hot for huge hits and pierce, but tip past the edge and you crash. Stay too cold and you crash the other way. The whole game is a rhythm puzzle hiding inside a fast, satisfying brawler, with 191+ cards, 33 relics, 10 heroes, and 38 enemies (9 of them bosses) shuffled across six handcrafted biomes.

What makes RH2 sing is co-op. Pull up a friend on the couch for **local 2-player** on a single keyboard, or rally up to **four players online** through real WebRTC peer-to-peer netplay — no servers, no accounts, just a 6-character room code. Sync your tempo zones with your party and trigger **Group Resonance** for stacking damage bonuses. Place markers your allies can detonate. Revive downed friends mid-fight. Equip **Pact relics** that only kick in when the squad plays as one. Solo runs feel sharp; four-player runs feel like a band finding its groove.

Under the hood it's a love letter to lean web tech: pure HTML5 Canvas, vanilla ES modules, zero build step, packaged as a tiny Electron app or playable straight in a browser. It runs buttery at 60 FPS on whatever potato you've got. Pick a hero, hit a seed, and find out how long you can stay in the pocket.

**Try it. The first crash is free.**
